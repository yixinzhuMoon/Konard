South Fiord Project

fast_forward

South Fiord Dome property is located on the western region of Axel Helberg Island, Nunavut Province, Canada. The zone covers an area of 22,726 acres and has completed its first stage of exploration. Being situated within the northern arctic circle, the activities at the mining site can be restricted by its weather extremities. On the other hand, this barren piece of land bears some of the rarest materials on the Earth at significantly high concentrations compared to other operating mining sites. Two outstanding types of rare minerals found at the mining site are gold and platinum group element (PGE) metals.

North Pole Mining LTD would obtain 43-101 National Instrument certificate for its South Fiord Dome Project to execute the official mining in Canadian Jurisdiction. An asset-backed token which represents its HEAD ORES will be issued through Konrad.
